Feta Pasta


Recipe description

This recipe became popular on tiktok in 2020, during the covid lockdown. It became increasingly popular and originated from Finladn. It was reported, that during this time feta cheese was completely sold-out.

Ingredients

  • Feta cheese
  • Pasta
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Prepare a baking dish with cut tomatoes and put eta cheese in the middle
  2. Season with olive oil, garlic, peper, salt, seasonings
  3. Put to bake in 200 celcius degrees for 40 min
  4. Make pasta in the meantime
  5. WHen tomatoes are done, combine and season with cheese or sauces. Enjoy.
