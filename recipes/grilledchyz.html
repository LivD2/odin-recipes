Grilled cheese


Recipe description

This recipe is a classic. A great snack for any time of the day, but especcially a great night snack.

Ingredients

  • Any cheese you like
  • Any bread
  • Optional - butter

Steps

  1. Prepare bread and cheese
  2. Fry bread in a pan with butter till golden brown
  3. Put cheese on both slices of bread
  4. When cheese melts, combine bread
